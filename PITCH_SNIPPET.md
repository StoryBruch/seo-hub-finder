# Short Application/Pitch Snippet

I built a small prototype that analyzes Google Search Console exports, automatically detects repeatable Programmatic SEO patterns, validates demand through a search-volume CSV, and turns the result into content hubs, article templates and internal linking plans.

You can test it without setup through the Streamlit demo: open the app and click `Try demo data`.

The important part: the tool does not require a manually predefined list of SEO patterns. It discovers repeatable structures from the GSC export itself and then uses search volume as a validation gate.
